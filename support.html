Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find deals near me?

Flash Hour uses your location to show you the best restaurant deals in your area. Make sure to enable location services in your device settings and the app will automatically display nearby deals and happy hours.

How do I save a deal?

Simply tap the heart icon on any deal to save it for later. You can view all your saved deals in the "Saved" tab at the bottom of the app.

How do I follow a restaurant?

Tap the star icon on any restaurant to follow them. You'll receive notifications when they post new deals or happy hours.

How do I redeem a deal?

Show the deal to the restaurant staff when you visit. Each deal will have specific terms and conditions that you'll need to follow for redemption.

I'm a restaurant owner. How do I create deals?

Restaurant owners can create deals through the Flash Hour dashboard. Contact us at business@flashhour.app to get started with your restaurant account.

How do I update my location?

The app automatically updates your location when you move. You can also manually refresh by pulling down on the main screen or going to Settings and enabling location services.

Getting Started

  1. Download the App: Get Flash Hour from the App Store
  2. Create an Account: Sign up with your email address
  3. Enable Location: Allow location access to see nearby deals
  4. Browse Deals: Explore deals and happy hours in your area
  5. Save & Follow: Save deals you like and follow restaurants
  6. Visit & Save: Show deals to restaurants to redeem them
